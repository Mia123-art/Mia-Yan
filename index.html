About Me

Curious by nature, creative by choice.

Growing through performance, collaboration, movement, and the courage to keep exploring.

Hi! I’m Mia Yitong Yan, a rising sophomore born and raised in Beijing, China, and currently studying at Beijing World Youth Academy, an IB school. I am a curious and well-rounded student whose interests span filmmaking, stage performance, dance, sports, and public speaking. I began ballet at the age of three and have participated in drama productions for more than ten years, experiences that shaped my confidence, discipline, imagination, and love for storytelling.

Outside the arts, I enjoy tennis, volleyball, skateboarding, snowboarding, and trying new experiences that challenge me to grow. I am still discovering the passion I hope to pursue for a lifetime, and I believe meaningful direction does not need to be rushed. For now, I want to remain open-minded, keep creating, and learn from every environment, team, and story I encounter.

My Experience

Every project is a new way to connect.

At BWYA, I take part in creative, athletic, and communication-focused communities. I help lead WolfBites, the school’s news-delivery channel, where I contribute to sharing stories and information with the wider school community. I also participate in film projects and build a growing collection of past films and creative profiles.

My background also includes acting in the BWYA school musical, competing as a member of the volleyball and football teams, and participating in public-speaking events, including TEDx-related experiences. Together, these opportunities have taught me how to perform with confidence, collaborate under pressure, communicate ideas clearly, and stay committed to a team.
